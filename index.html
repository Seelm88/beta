🐸 Welcome to the Frontlines

The 513th Frog Battalion isn't just another War Thunder squadron—we're a community of tactical thinkers, competitive players, and meme-loving warriors who take teamwork seriously... but not ourselves.

Whether we're pushing a capture point in a coordinated blitz or sharing the latest "Skill Issue" moment in Discord, the goal is the same: having fun together.

Why join the Battalion?

  • ✔ Coordinated SRE/SQB: Experience high-level tactical gameplay with a team that focuses on both results and camaraderie.
  • ✔ Events & Competitions: Participate in our internal tournaments and unique events where glory (and sometimes prizes) are on the line.
  • ✔ Dedicated Servers: We provide stable, dedicated servers to ensure the best possible gaming experience for our members.
  • ✔ Brotherhood (and Memes): Our Discord is the heart of the battalion—a place for strategy, support, and superior frog-tier humor.

Whether you’re a hardened veteran with a full hangar or a new recruit looking for a reliable wingman, you’ve just found your unit. Suit up, hop in, and let's cause some havoc.
